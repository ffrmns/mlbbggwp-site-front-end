Belerick is a nice to play hero. He have the passive skill that is enough to counter fighter and marksman. He can run with his second skill. Ultimate and first skill will give damage and slow.

As usual, it needs the tank builds. If the enemy has no magical damage, don't buy magical defense items. Usually, my physical defense item is assault cuirass, beccause it will reduce the attacker damage. Also, it's nice to have blade armor so the enemies basic attack will bounced back to them.
